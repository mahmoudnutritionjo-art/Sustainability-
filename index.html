Sustainability & Responsibility

Building a Responsible Future

At First Nutrition, sustainability is about creating lasting value—for our customers, employees, partners, and the communities we serve.

As one of the Middle East and North Africa's leading distributors of premium sports nutrition brands, we recognize that long-term success depends on operating responsibly, maintaining the highest quality standards, protecting our people, and continuously improving the way we do business.

Our approach to sustainability is built on three core pillars:

Environmental Stewardship Social Responsibility Corporate Governance

Our approach to sustainability is built on three core pillars: Environmental Stewardship, Social Responsibility, and Corporate Governance. These commitments are supported by internationally recognized management systems that provide an independent framework for excellence, accountability, and continuous improvement.

Framework

Our ESG Commitment

Environmental Stewardship

We are committed to minimizing the environmental impact of our operations while continuously improving efficiency throughout our supply chain.

Although our business focuses on the importation and distribution of premium nutrition products rather than manufacturing, we recognize our responsibility to operate sustainably by:

  • Optimizing warehousing and inventory management to minimize waste.
  • Improving logistics planning to enhance transportation efficiency.
  • Encouraging digital processes to reduce unnecessary paper consumption.
  • Continuously evaluating operational practices to improve environmental performance.
  • Partnering with internationally recognized brands that share our commitment to responsible business practices.

We believe that responsible environmental management is an ongoing journey, and we remain committed to identifying new opportunities to improve our environmental performance as our business grows.

Social Responsibility

People are the foundation of our success.

We are committed to creating a workplace that values integrity, equal opportunity, respect, safety, and professional development while building lasting relationships with customers, suppliers, and business partners.

Our commitment includes:

  • Promoting equal opportunity and fair employment practices.
  • Maintaining a safe and healthy working environment.
  • Conducting business with honesty, transparency, and integrity.
  • Respecting diversity, inclusion, and human rights.
  • Investing in employee learning and professional development.
  • Building trusted partnerships through ethical business practices.

These principles guide our decisions every day and reinforce our commitment to creating positive value for everyone we work with.

Corporate Governance

Strong governance is essential to maintaining trust and delivering consistent performance.

Our management systems ensure that responsibilities are clearly defined, risks are effectively managed, and business processes are continuously monitored and improved.

Our governance framework focuses on:

  • Ethical leadership and responsible decision-making.
  • Compliance with applicable legal and regulatory requirements.
  • Quality assurance through standardized management systems.
  • Risk management and continuous improvement.
  • Transparent communication with customers, suppliers, and stakeholders.

By embedding accountability into every aspect of our operations, we strengthen long-term partnerships and support sustainable business growth.

Independent Validation

International Certifications

Our commitment to responsible business is supported by internationally recognized ISO standards that independently validate the way we manage quality, social responsibility, and occupational health and safety.

Quality Management System

Our ISO 9001 certified Quality Management System ensures that every stage of our operations—from supplier qualification and product handling to warehousing, logistics, and customer service—is managed through standardized, documented, and continuously improving processes.

This enables us to deliver:

  • ✓ Consistent product handling and operational excellence.
  • ✓ Accurate order processing and dependable delivery.
  • ✓ Controlled storage and inventory management.
  • ✓ Reliable customer service.
  • ✓ Continuous improvement across all business functions.

Quality is embedded into every process to ensure our customers receive dependable products and services they can trust.

Looking Ahead

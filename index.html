Sustainability & Responsibility

Building a Responsible Future

At First Nutrition, sustainability is about creating lasting value—for our customers, employees, partners, and the communities we serve.

As one of the Middle East and North Africa's leading distributors of premium sports nutrition brands, we recognize that long-term success depends on operating responsibly, maintaining the highest quality standards, and continuously improving the way we do business.

Our approach to sustainability is built on three core pillars:

Environmental Stewardship Social Responsibility Corporate Governance

These commitments are supported by internationally recognized management systems that provide an independent framework for excellence, accountability, and continuous improvement.

Framework

Our ESG Commitment

Environmental Stewardship

We are committed to minimizing the environmental impact of our operations while continuously improving efficiency throughout our supply chain by:

  • Optimizing warehousing and inventory management to minimize waste.
  • Improving logistics planning to enhance transportation efficiency.
  • Encouraging digital processes to reduce paper consumption.
  • Partnering with global brands that share our sustainable values.
Supply Chain & Efficiency

Social Responsibility

People are the foundation of our success. We value integrity, equal opportunity, respect, safety, and professional development:

  • Promoting equal opportunity & fair employment.
  • Maintaining a safe and healthy working environment.
  • Investing in employee learning & development.
  • Conducting business with transparency and integrity.
People & Culture First

Corporate Governance

Strong governance is essential to maintaining trust. Our systems ensure clear responsibilities and managed risks:

  • Ethical leadership and responsible decision-making.
  • Compliance with global legal and regulatory requirements.
  • Quality assurance through standardized frameworks.
  • Transparent communication with all stakeholders.
